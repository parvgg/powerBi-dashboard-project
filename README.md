📊 Data Jobs Dashboard (Power BI) – Version 2.0
📌 Introduction

Navigating the data job market can be overwhelming due to the vast amount of scattered information available online. This Data Jobs Dashboard (V2.0) is designed to simplify that process for job seekers, career transitioners, and professionals exploring new opportunities.

Using a real-world dataset of 2024 data science job postings, this Power BI project provides a single-page interactive dashboard that highlights key insights such as job demand, salary trends, and skill requirements. The goal is to enable users to quickly analyze the data job market and make informed career decisions.

📍 Dashboard Overview (Version 2.0)

Version 2.0 focuses on a single-page dashboard layout to provide the most important job market insights in one place.

This page functions as a central control panel for analyzing the data job market, presenting:

📊 Job Count – Total number of job postings
🧠 Skills per Job – Average skills required
💰 Median Yearly Salary
⏱️ Median Hourly Salary

Users can also explore:
-Skill popularity (by percentage or count)
-Salary comparisons across job titles
-Location-based job distribution
-The design prioritizes clarity, efficiency, and quick insight discovery.
